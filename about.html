Who We Are

Born From a Love of Flowers

It started with a small market stall and a passion for bringing joy through flowers. Today, Flowers is a beloved local institution trusted by thousands of families, couples, and event planners across the city.

We partner directly with sustainable farms within a 50-mile radius, ensuring every stem arrives at your door at its absolute peak. No warehouses, no middlemen — just the freshest blooms, chosen with care each morning.

Our team of eight florists brings a combined experience of over 60 years in floral design, wedding styling, and botanical art. We treat every arrangement as a small work of art.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Sustainability

We source exclusively from certified sustainable farms and use 100% biodegradable packaging. Every purchase plants a tree through our partnership with local reforestation programs.

Craftsmanship

No two arrangements are identical. Our florists hand-craft every bouquet to order, selecting each stem individually for color, form, and freshness. It's slow work done with pride.

Community

We donate unsold flowers to local hospitals and care homes every evening. Because beauty should be shared. We're not just a flower shop — we're a neighborhood institution.
